Lideta Sub City

Lideta sub-city is one of the 11/eleven/ sub-cities in Addis Ababa, the capital city of Ethiopia, and it is located below Stephen's Church and next to Mariwot Hotel. It is a service-providing government institution that includes 10/ten/ woredas. As of 2011 its population was of 235,441.

Vision

Seeing Addis Ababa as a symbol of Africa's prosperity

Mission

By strengthening internal and external relations, by increasing the construction of the city's appearance, by monitoring and evaluating the performance of institutions based on results, by implementing cabinet decisions that ensure the universal benefit of the people, and by establishing good governance, by making service delivery fair, transparent and accountable, it is to ensure the benefit of the society.

Core Values

      . Loyalty
      . Motivation
      . Excellent service
      . Rule of law
      . Greater public interest
      . Leading with knowledge and faith
